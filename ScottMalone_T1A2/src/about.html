Rugby Union.

Since I was 20 years old I made the conversion from playing rugby league to rugby union. I developed lifelong friendships and a culture that only a rugby player will understand. I gained the ability to captain many sides to victory. Aside for the many health benifits playing rugby has also developed many skills such as working in a team, making quick decisive decisions, leadership skills, comradery and many more life skills.

Surfing.

If I am not playing rugby I am surfing. Growing up around beaches since a child I have grown a passion for the ocean and surfing. I have lived within 100m off a beach for 2/3's of my life. Every trip I go on including overseas required some surfing motive behind every decision to travel. I have also had the opportunity to go scuba diving. Travelling to fiji I had the opportunity to go scuba diving with 50+ bull sharks swimming around me without a cage.

Hiking.

Meeting my girlfriend and now mother to be, I was influenced to start hiking or bush walking. Discovering the planet outside of technology and away from the influences of society I find it very serene and also a way to keep healthy.
